Fried Rice

Very nice easy friend rice recipe

Ingredients

  • Cooked Rice
  • Peas and Carrots
  • Green Onion
  • Soy Sauce
  • Diced Onion
  • Seasame Oil
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook veggies on medium heat, add seasame oil, chopped white onion, and frozen peas and carrots, cook till tender
  2. Cook eggs: slide veggies to the side and pour eggs on other side of skillet. Scramble the eggs using a spatula. Mix with veggies once cooked
  3. Add rice on top and soy sauce. Mix them together
  4. Enjoy!!!
