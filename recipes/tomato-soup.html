Tomato Soup

Easy tomato soup recipe with only 3 ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes (10 to 12 medium or 2 pounds)
  • Half a large onion cut into large wedges
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Put all ingredients into a pot
  2. Simmer on low heat for 40 minutes
  3. Blend until smooth
  4. Enjoy!!!
