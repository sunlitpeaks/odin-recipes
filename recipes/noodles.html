Aweseom stir fry noodles recipes

Ingredients

  • Fish Sauce
  • Kecap manis (sweet soy)
  • Hokkien Noodles
  • Chicken Thighs
  • Green cabbage
  • Green onion

Steps

  1. Mix kecap manis and fish sauce together until the manis is dissolved
  2. Toss 1 tbs of the sauce with the chicken to flavor the surface
  3. Prepare the noodles based on packet instructions, then give rinse under tap water and drain excess water
  4. Heat oil until smoking, then cook chicken for 2 minutes, then toss garlic and white parts of green onion
  5. Add cabbage and cooked for around 1:30 minutes
  6. Then add noodles and half of the sauce, tossing until the sauce is absorbed, repeat with other half. Then toss chili sauce and green part of the green onion, cook for another 20 seconds
  7. Enjoy!!!
